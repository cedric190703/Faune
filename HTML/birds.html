Birds :

The proverb "who looks alike comes together" is true. In this section, we present a sample of the 462 bird species found in Canada.

And where, in Canada, do most birds congregate? In British Columbia, where you can find 362 species, and in Ontario, where 318 species occur regularly.

Many of these species migrate to find suitable breeding grounds, more favourable temperatures and winter food. Some of them travel long distances.
