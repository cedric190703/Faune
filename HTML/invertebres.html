invertebrates :

Invertebrates are animals that do not have a spine. They are very varied and include very different species from each other, for example, the terrestrial slug, the green sea urchin, the marine sponges and the spider.

In Canada, approximately 35,000 species of insects have been identified. We should therefore not be surprised at the essential role they play in maintaining the health of our ecosystem and our environment.

Birds, fish and frogs depend on insects as an invaluable source of food. We humans depend on insects for honey, beeswax and silk. Our food crops, flowers and plants could not multiply without the pollen that intrepid insects bring them. Insects are also predators of pests that can ravage our gardens.
